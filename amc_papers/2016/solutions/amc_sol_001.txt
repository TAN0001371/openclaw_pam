**Solutions – MiddlePrimary Division – 2016**

**1.** 20 + 16 = 36,
hence (C).

**2.** (Also UP1)
The numbers in order are 555, 556, 565, 566, 655,
hence (D).

**3.** The digit 3 is in the thousands position, so it represents 3000,
hence (D).

**4.** I am 12 years old, so our combined ages are 6 + 12 = 18,
hence (C).

**5.** Here are all possible lines of symmetry:
[Diagram: A row of five capital letters: V, W, X, Y, Z. 
- The V has a single vertical line of symmetry drawn through its center.
- The W has a single vertical line of symmetry drawn through its center.
- The X has both a horizontal and a vertical line of symmetry drawn through its center.
- The Y has a single vertical line of symmetry drawn through its center.
- The Z has no line of symmetry drawn.]
Although the Z shape has a point of symmetry, it does not have a line of symmetry,
hence (E).

**6.** (Also UP2)
One pizza will have 4 quarters, so two pizzas will have 2 × 4 = 8 quarters,
hence (D).

**7.** After 30 minutes it is 5 pm, and after another 15 minutes it is 5:15 pm,
hence (E).

**8.** The number of wheels is 4 × 2 + 2 × 3 + 1 × 4 = 18,
hence (E).

**9.** (Also UP6)
The opposite chair is both 5 places forward and 5 places back.
Five places back from chair 9 is chair 4,
[Diagram: A circle representing chairs numbered 1 through 10 in a clockwise arrangement. An arrow curves along the circle, starting from chair 9 and pointing towards chair 4.]
hence (D).

**10.** (Also UP5)
Alternative 1
In cents, 500 ÷ 80 = 6r20 so that he buys 6 chocolates and has 20 cents left,
hence (C).
