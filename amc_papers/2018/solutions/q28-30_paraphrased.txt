### Problem 28: Staircase Blocks
*   **Question:** Determine the total number of blocks required to build a staircase with 12 steps, where the first step is 1 block high, the second is 2 blocks high, and so on, up to the 12th step which is 12 blocks high.
*   **Mathematical Approach:** 
    *   One method involves calculating the sum of the triangular numbers for each slice, represented as the sum of integers from 1 to 12 ($78$), multiplied by the 12 slices, resulting in $12 \times 78$.
    *   An alternative method involves rearranging the staircase into a rectangular prism with dimensions $6 \times 12 \times 13$.
*   **Answer:** 936 blocks.
