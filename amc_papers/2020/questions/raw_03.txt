Questions 21 to 25, 5 marks each

21. After the first kilometre of the school cross-country run, Petra was second last.
In the next kilometre she managed to overtake seven runners.
In the third kilometre, two runners overtook her. In the final kilometre, she passed eight runners, but four other runners overtook her.
She finished ninth.
How many were in the race?
(A) 15 (B) 18 (C) 19 (D) 20 (E) 21

22. I fold up this net to make a cube.
I then multiply the numbers on opposite faces to get three numbers.
The largest of these is
(A) 12 (B) 15 (C) 18 (D) 24 (E) 30

23. Emanuel works in a busy restaurant washing dishes. Each dirty plate from the stack on the left takes 1 minute to wash and dry, before being placed on top of the clean stack on the right. After 7 minutes, and every 7 minutes from then on, a waiter brings 4 more dirty plates and adds them to the top of the dirty stack.

[Diagram description for Question 23: Two stacks of white plates. The left stack is tall and labeled "15 dirty plates", with a darker coloured plate inserted near the bottom of the stack. The right stack is shorter and labeled "3 clean plates".]

How high is the stack of clean plates when the coloured plate is being washed?
(A) 14 (B) 16 (C) 18 (D) 20 (E) 22
