Third day = x + 10

Fifth day = x + 20

x + (x + 5) + (x + 10) + (x + 15) + (x + 20) = 100
5x + 50 = 100
5x = 100 - 50
x = 50 / 5 = 10

So she saved 10 + 20 = 30¢ on fifth day. Hence (C)
