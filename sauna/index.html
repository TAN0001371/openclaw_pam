About

Not a Kit.
Never Will Be.

Walk into a hardware store, and you'll find sauna kits. Flat-packed. Made overseas. Generic. That's not what we do.

Every Melbourne Prestige Sauna is handmade right here in Victoria using premium western red cedar — selected for its natural resistance to moisture, its intoxicating aroma, and its unmistakable warmth. No particle board. No shortcuts. No two builds are the same, because no two clients are the same.

We sit down with you. We measure your space. We design every detail — from timber grade and bench height to lighting placement and door hardware. Then we build it. By hand. On site. Your sauna, your way.

  • Premium Canadian western red cedar — clear grade, kiln-dried
  • Hand-built in Melbourne by a dedicated craftsman, not a production line
  • Every dimension, every fixture, every finish — your choice
  • Designed for Australian conditions and built to last decades
